LION

(Panthera leo),is a large and powerful built cat that is second in size only to the tiger. Lions are most active at night and live in a variety of habitats.

PUMAS

It has a small and broad head with a high, arched forehead. They have a slender, elongated body with powerful limbs, pumas typically live 15-18 years

HIPPOPOTAMUS

Hippos are large semi-aquatic mammals, with large barrel-shaped body, short legs and tail, greyish to muddy-brown skin , and an enormous head.

CHEETAH

It is a lightly built, spotted cat characterised by a small rounded head, a short snout, black tear-like facial streaks, a deep chest, long thin legs and a long tail.

APE

Apes are distinguished from monkeys by the complete absence of a tail and the presence of an appendix and by their more complex brains.

BABOON

They all have dog-like noses, powerful jaws, sharp canine teeth, and thick fur. Males have a longer mane around the neck, called a ruff.
